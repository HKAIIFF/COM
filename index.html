Frequently Asked Questions

What is AIF-HKD?

AIF-HKD is the core stablecoin of the Hong Kong AI International Film Festival ecosystem, maintaining a 1:1 peg with the Hong Kong Dollar. It provides stable, efficient, and compliant digital payment solutions for the AI film industry.

We use a 100% fiat-collateralized model where every AIF-HKD issued has corresponding HKD reserves. Reserve assets are held at Hong Kong licensed banks and subject to regular audits.

Is AIF-HKD compliant?

Yes, AIF-HKD fully complies with Hong Kong Monetary Authority stablecoin regulatory requirements, following Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and Know Your Customer (KYC) compliance standards.

How can I get AIF-HKD?

Users can obtain AIF-HKD by exchanging HKD at a 1:1 ratio through official wallets, partner exchanges, or authorized service providers. The exchange process requires KYC verification completion.

What are the use cases for AIF-HKD?

AIF-HKD can be used for film project financing, creator revenue settlement, education and training payments, content consumption, submission service fees, and many other scenarios.

How are reserve assets secured?

Reserve assets are securely held by compliant custodial institutions, using multi-signature, cold storage and other security measures, with regular independent audits by third-party audit firms.
