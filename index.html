// The Foundation

AIF-HKD is the world's first stablecoin designed specifically for the AI film industry. Based on the regulatory framework of the Hong Kong Monetary Authority's "Stablecoin Ordinance", providing a stable digital financial infrastructure for the HKAIIFF ecosystem. AIF-HKD是全球首款專為AI電影產業設計的穩定幣，立足香港金管局《穩定幣條例》的合規框架，為HKAIIFF生態系統提供穩定的數字金融基礎設施。

FAQ常見問題

What is AIF-HKD?什麼是 AIF-HKD？ ▼
AIF-HKD is the world's first HKD-pegged stablecoin designed for the AI film industry. It aims to be the core digital financial infrastructure for the AI-native film industry, offering stable, efficient, and compliant solutions for cross-border transactions and settlements. AIF-HKD是全球首款專為AI電影產業設計的港幣錨定穩定幣，與港幣保持1:1的錨定關係。它旨在成為AI原生電影產業的核心數字金融基礎設施，為跨境交易、數字資產交易和透明結算提供穩定、高效且合規的解決方案。
How does it differ from other stablecoins?與其他穩定幣有何不同？ ▼
Unlike general stablecoins like USDT/USDC, AIF-HKD is vertically specialized for the AI film industry, deeply integrated with the HKAIIFF ecosystem. It offers specific financial services like project financing and copyright trading, backed by HKMA's regulatory framework. AIF-HKD專為AI電影產業設計，深度整合HKAIIFF生態系統。與USDT、USDC等通用穩定幣不同，我們專注於垂直領域應用，提供特定的金融服務如項目融資、版權交易、跨境征片結算等。同時，我們擁有香港金管局的明確監管框架支持。
How do you ensure stability?如何確保穩定性和安全性？ ▼
AIF-HKD uses a fiat-collateralized model, 100% backed by high-quality, liquid HKD assets. We maintain over-collateralization, daily reserve monitoring, monthly independent audits, and a transparent proof-of-reserve system. AIF-HKD採用法定貨幣抵押模式，100%由高質量、流動性強的港幣計價資產提供支持。我們建立了超額抵押機制、每日儲備監控、月度獨立審計和公開透明的儲備證明系統，確保絕對的穩定性和安全性。
