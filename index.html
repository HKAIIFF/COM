Licensing Regime

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about AIF-HKD to help you better understand our stablecoin project.

What is AIF-HKD?
AIF-HKD is the fiat-pegged stablecoin of the Hong Kong AI International Film Festival (HKAIIFF) ecosystem, maintaining a 1:1 peg with the Hong Kong Dollar. It aims to serve as the core digital financial infrastructure for the AI-native film industry, providing stable, efficient, and compliant solutions for cross-border transactions, digital asset trading, and transparent settlements.

How does AIF-HKD ensure stability?
AIF-HKD adopts a fiat-collateralized model, backed 100% by high-quality, liquid HKD-denominated assets including cash and short-term government bonds. We have established over-collateralization mechanisms, daily reserve monitoring, monthly independent audits, and publicly transparent reserve proof systems to ensure absolute stability.

What about regulatory compliance?
AIF-HKD strictly adheres to HKMA's Stablecoin Ordinance (effective August 1, 2025) and will apply for a stablecoin issuer license. We have established comprehensive AML/KYC procedures, data protection mechanisms, and continuous compliance monitoring systems to ensure legal operations in Hong Kong and major global markets.

How to mint and redeem AIF-HKD?
Minting: Users deposit HKD to designated bank accounts, and after bank confirmation, smart contracts automatically mint equivalent AIF-HKD and send to user wallets. Redemption: Users submit redemption requests and burn AIF-HKD, smart contracts verify and release equivalent HKD from reserves to user's designated bank account. The entire process is transparent, automated, and efficient.

How is AIF-HKD different from other stablecoins?
AIF-HKD is specifically designed for the AI film industry and deeply integrated with the HKAIIFF ecosystem. Unlike general-purpose stablecoins like USDT and USDC, we focus on vertical applications, providing specific financial services such as project financing, copyright trading, and cross-border film submission settlements. Additionally, we have clear regulatory framework support from HKMA.

What are the future development plans?
We will develop in four phases: Infrastructure Development (Q3-Q4 2025), Ecosystem Integration (Q1-Q2 2026), Global Expansion (Q3 2026-2027), and Innovation Development (2027+). We plan to enter major markets including the US, EU, Singapore, and Japan, and explore CBDC integration, programmable finance, and real-world asset tokenization innovations.
