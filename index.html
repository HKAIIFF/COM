What is AIF-HKD

AIF-HKD is the world's first stablecoin designed specifically for the AI film industry, operating within Hong Kong Monetary Authority's (HKMA) regulatory framework to provide a stable digital financial infrastructure for the HKAIIFF ecosystem.

Stable & Trustworthy

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about AIF-HKD to help you better understand our stablecoin project.

What is AIF-HKD?
AIF-HKD is the world's first HKD-pegged stablecoin designed specifically for the AI film industry, maintaining a 1:1 peg with the Hong Kong Dollar. It aims to become the core digital financial infrastructure for the AI-native film industry, providing a stable, efficient, and compliant solution for cross-border transactions, digital asset trading, and transparent settlements.

How is AIF-HKD different from other stablecoins?
AIF-HKD is designed specifically for the AI film industry and deeply integrated with the HKAIIFF ecosystem. Unlike general-purpose stablecoins like USDT and USDC, we focus on vertical industry applications, providing specialized financial services such as project financing, copyright trading, and cross-border film submission settlements. Additionally, we benefit from HKMA's clear regulatory framework support, giving us a first-mover advantage.

What is the regulatory compliance status?
AIF-HKD strictly complies with the Hong Kong Monetary Authority's Stablecoin Ordinance (effective August 1, 2025) and will apply for a stablecoin issuer license. We have established comprehensive AML/KYC procedures, data protection mechanisms, and ongoing compliance monitoring systems to ensure legal operations in Hong Kong and other major global markets.

How is stability and security ensured?
AIF-HKD employs a fiat currency collateral model, 100% backed by high-quality, liquid HKD-denominated assets. We have established an overcollateralization mechanism, daily reserve monitoring, monthly independent audits, and a transparent reserve proof system to ensure absolute stability and security.

How can I participate in the AIF-HKD ecosystem?
You can participate in multiple ways: as an AI film creator using AIF-HKD for project financing, as an investor participating in film project investments, as a user trading NFTs on AIF.Market, or as a student paying course fees at HKAIFA. We will provide complete onboarding guides and technical support.
